Eric J. Taipale

Overview

My work has appeared in print and online, and has been translated into multiple languages, with my work appearing in Discover New Scientist, Gehirn & Geist, and Psychology Today. At Psychology Today, I author the column called Young Minds and Crime, which explores key issues in child and adolescent forensic psychology. Since September 2023, I have been pursuing a Bachelor of Arts (Honors) with a major in psychology at Queen’s University in Kingston, Ontario.
